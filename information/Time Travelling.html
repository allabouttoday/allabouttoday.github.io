Time Travelling

Time travel is travelling between two points in time. It may be going ahead into the future or back into the past. It remains a dream for scientists to travel in time. Most of them are trying to develop a time machine to do so.
But till now no one has succeeded in doing so. Not all researchers and scientists believe that it is possible for humans to travel in time. Scientific theories suggest it's possible to travel in time. But in reality it may not be.

History

Time travel has been nearly impossible for many scientists for at least a century (120 years). Back in 1895, when H.G well described the time travel in his groundbreaking novel, 'The Time Machine'. It was mostly describing the physicists and philosophers writing serious research papers about time travel.At the end of the 19th century, scientists started investigations and described time as a dimension, just like space. If we can go anywhere in space and then similarly we can go in time. Some d the theory that describe the time travel are describe below.
