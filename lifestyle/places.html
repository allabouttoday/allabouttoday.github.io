Best 5 places to visit before you die

Got a travel craze or are you trying to fill out your bucket list with places to visit? Maybe you're just tired of everyday life and need an escape. If you're wondering what places you should start with, we've got it covered for you. Here are the best 5 places you need to visit before you die that should be kept on top of your list.

Switzerland

This Central European country often called the country of romance, has the most scenic places that calm the mind and caress the soul. Whether it's a getaway you're planning with your lover or for yourself, Switzerland has both breathtaking, beautiful scenery and activities to offer. While Switzerland offers the usual activities like skydiving, sledging, skiing, paragliding, etc., it also offers some very unique activities. Here is a list of some of these calming sceneries and quirky activities

Scenic Beauties

  • Jungfraujoch- Also known as “The top of Europe”.
  • Ticino and Lake Lugano
  • Lauterbrunnen- An amazing blend of nature and man with a village thriving beautifully in the valleys of the Alps.
  • Bern- A UNESCO world heritage and the capital of Switzerland offers the chance to see pretty scenery and the tallest cathedral in Switzerland.
  • Lake Geneva- Typically visited by most for the view and Jet D’Eau

Unique activities

  • Sky Dining- Yes, you read that correctly. A three-course meal that's served when you're in a cable-car gondola that gifts you with the beauty of Lake Lucerne while climbing up Mount Rig
  • Wine Tasting- For wine lovers, do head out into the vineyards for some fine local wine.
  • The Bear Park- A park where you can play and interact with bears

Bali

Along with being called “The Island of Gods”, Bali is known for its historical monuments and lush greenery. It is one of the places anyone should visit at least once in their lifetime. The charming greenery and intricately constructed temples are a must have experience.

Scenic Beauties

  • Penglipuran Village- A village untouched by modernization
  • Banyumala Twin waterfalls- Two waterfalls together.
  • Bamboo Forest in Bangli- Arched bamboo trees forming a tunnel over a pathway
  • Komodo Island- It has one of the seven pink beaches on the planet.

Temples and monuments

  • The “Doors of Heaven” at Pura Lempuyang- The door-like structure is definitely one of the most photogenic spots
  • Pura Tirta Empul- The pool in the temple is said to spiritually cleanse you
  • Tanah Lot Temple- Visit the cafe near it and get amazing pictures with the ocean view!

Italy

There is simply so much to see in Italy that it’s hard to list out only a few spots. The historical buildings, the architecture, and the scenery give a rivalry to all the tourist spots in the world. The entire country is a beauty in itself which is why we will, unfortunately, list out only a few of our favorite places and monuments

Scenic beauties

  • Tuscan Hill Towns- Stone towns surrounded by greenery that have their base in history
  • Lake Como- One of the best summer retreats
  • Naples
  • Sardinia

    • Monuments

    • Il Duomo- Milan Cathedral
    • Leaning Tower of Pisa and Town Lucca
    • Pompeii and Herculaneum- Excavated cities engulfed by volcanic eruptions in history
    • Siena and Florence

Greece

Yet another country of beauty and history, Greece offers tons of monumental sites of which 18 are branded as World Heritage sites. It provides views of heart-stealing landscapes and cities with an ocean backdrop.

Scenic Beauties

  • Santorini- Best sunset and ocean view
  • Thessaloniki- Has the best nightlife that is considered unmatched.
  • Meteora- Cliffs rising more than 1200 feet that overlook the Kalambaka and Kastraki villages
  • Crete- Spectacular landscapes

Monuments

  • Cape Sounion- Ruins of the ancient temple of the Greek God of water, Poseidon
  • The ancient monument of Delphi
  • Acropolis of Athens
  • Dion- A sanctuary that was dedicated to Zeus

Egypt

Offering desert views, camel rides, and monuments along with other activities, Egypt is one of the best places to visit. Whether its monuments who have that ancient feel to them or unique activities, Egypt is bound to intrigue you as a whole.

Monuments

  • Luxor Temple- Visiting both in the morning and at night is recommended.
  • Sakkara- An ancient burial ground
  • Pyramids of Giza- Vast and remarkable, the Pyramids of Giza are a site to see.
  • Great Sphinx- You already know you’re going to go there to get the all-time kissing photo with the Great Sphinx.
  • Abu Simbel- A temple carved into the rocks

Activities

  • Sail down the Nile to take its beauty in the best way possible
  • A Hot air balloon ride in Luxor will give you the chance to take in the city’s beauty from up in the air
  • Nuban village- Visit the Nubians and have dinner with them. The village is so colourful and you can get handicrafts as well as spices from the market stalls.
  • Eat Falafel sandwiches.

These are the places we leave as a strong suggestion for you to visit. The beauty, culture, and architecture in these countries are undeniable and are just as hypnotizing to a normal traveler as it is to travel junkies. So what are you waiting for? Take your pick and start packing!
